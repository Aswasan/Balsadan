Our Curriculum

At Bal Sadan Academy, we follow a comprehensive curriculum that aims to develop students' academic knowledge, critical thinking skills, creativity, and character. Our curriculum is aligned with national educational standards while incorporating innovative teaching methods.

We believe in providing a balanced education that prepares students not just for examinations, but for life. Our approach focuses on conceptual understanding rather than rote learning, encouraging students to explore, question, and discover.

Academic Levels

Primary School (Classes 1-5)

Our primary school program focuses on building a strong foundation in core subjects while nurturing curiosity and creativity. The curriculum is designed to be engaging and interactive, making learning enjoyable for young minds.

Core Subjects:

  • English Language and Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Regional Language

Co-Curricular Activities:

  • Art and Craft
  • Music and Dance
  • Physical Education
  • Computer Education
  • Value Education

Middle School (Classes 6-8)

The middle school program builds upon the foundation laid in primary school, introducing more advanced concepts and encouraging analytical thinking. Students are exposed to a wider range of subjects and activities to help them discover their interests and talents.

Core Subjects:

  • English Language and Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Science (History, Geography, Civics)
  • Regional Language
  • Third Language (Optional)

Co-Curricular Activities:

  • Advanced Computer Education
  • Sports and Games
  • Performing Arts
  • Literary Activities
  • Environmental Studies

Secondary School (Classes 9-10)

Our secondary school program prepares students for board examinations while developing critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a deeper understanding of subjects. The curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually and prepare them for higher education.

Core Subjects:

  • English Language and Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Science (History, Geography, Economics, Civics)
  • Regional Language

Additional Programs:

  • Career Counseling
  • Leadership Development
  • Community Service
  • Advanced Computer Applications
  • Life Skills Education
