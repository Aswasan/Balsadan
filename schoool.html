About Samriddhi School

Established in 2005, Samriddhi School has evolved into one of the premier educational institutions in Nepal. Our mission is to provide quality education that prepares students to excel academically and thrive in a rapidly changing world.

At Samriddhi, we believe that education goes beyond textbooks. Our integrated approach combines academic rigor with character building, critical thinking, and creativity.

With a team of highly qualified and experienced educators, we nurture each student's unique talents and abilities, helping them discover their potential and pursue their passions.
